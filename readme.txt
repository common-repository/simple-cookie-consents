Simple Cookie Consent is a lightweight wordpress plugin for alerting users about the use of cookies on your website. A free solution to the EU, GDPR, and California Cookie Laws. It is designed to help you quickly comply with the EU Cookie Law, CCPA, GDPR and other privacy laws. We made it fast, free, and relatively painless.

== Description ==

Simple Cookie Consent is a lightweight wordpress plugin for alerting users about the use of cookies on your website.

A free solution to the EU, GDPR, and California Cookie Laws

It is designed to help you quickly comply with the EU Cookie Law, CCPA, GDPR and other privacy laws.

We made it fast, free, and relatively painless

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin to your blog.
2. Activate it
3. Visit plugin settings and edit your privacy policy url

1, 2, 3: You're done!
